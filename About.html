About Me

My name is Ronabe Maquiling, and I am a BSIT student from the National Iloilo State University. I am passionate about advancing agricultural technology and creating innovative solutions for farmers. My goal is to use technology to solve real-world problems and promote sustainability.

Beyond academics, I find joy in immersing myself in artistic pursuits and connecting with nature. Whether it's sketching, painting, or simply exploring the outdoors, these activities serve as my sanctuary, offering me a chance to recharge and stay inspired. Engaging with nature not only helps me manage stress but also provides a peaceful escape that keeps me grounded amid life's challenges. Through a balance of technology, creativity, and a deep connection to the environment, I strive to make meaningful contributions to society while staying true to my values.
